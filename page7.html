CALL TRACKING

Our call tracking ability to trace phone calls back to the person who initiated the call, as well as the relevant advertising channel that prompted the call, is the newest addition to the Next Level Lead feature set! It works by assigning unique phone numbers to different advertising sources. When the prospect sees your ad, they call the phone number that is then forwarded to the designated phone number. Next Level Leads is then able to collect metrics about the call, record and transcribe the call if you would like, notify you by text or email, and many more options. This is truly Next Level technology that can help you maintain incoming calls from your marketing efforts. We can assign a phone number to your newspaper ad and another number to your social media ads to help you track which marketing techniques your using are working the best for your business!
